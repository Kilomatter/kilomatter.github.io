Tinhead, Percival, and Grough

Tinhead, Percival, and Grough (pronounced "Gruff") are a rival team on the same quest as Milo, Nico, and Gus. Tinhead is the shorter knight, and the hotheaded group leader. Percival is the sorry-looking fellow in the back who seems like he's only here out of obligation, and Grough is Tinhead's goat. Don't ask Tihead why he spelled it that way because it'll only make him madder! The three of them and Team Milo run into each other fairly often, and whenever they do, humorous chaos swiftly follows.
