Milo

Milo is our plucky little protagonist. In an attempt to save up enough gold to buy a dog, Milo teams up with the aspiring adventurer Nico on a quest to rescue Princess Miranda from the sinister Castle Grotesque. Milo is innocent, optimistic, brutally honest, and strong-willed. Milo is only a kid, so to help out with the long travel diatance, he rides around on his pig named Gus.
