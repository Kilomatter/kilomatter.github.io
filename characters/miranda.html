Princess Miranda

Princess Miranda, daughter of King Rubeus Pendragon, is the heir to the throne of the Pendragon Kingdom. However, recently she has been kidnapped by the sinister forces occupying Castle Grotesque. Fortunately, Milo and Nico are on their way to rescue her! Miranda is a serious, thoughtful character. She turns out to be an excellent tactician when she appears later on.
