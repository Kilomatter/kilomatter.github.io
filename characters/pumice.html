Pete and Pint Pumice

Pete and Pint are a father-son team that run a shop together in the Furrowfair Marketplace. Shopkeep Pete is the guy selling the dog that Milo wants to buy. Pete and Pint are both hunters, with Pete being a big game hunter, and Pint being a bug-catcher. Pete may seem intimindating due to his large size and strength, but he's actually a really nice guy.
